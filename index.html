CTU DANAO CAMPUS HIGHLIGHTS

Welcome to the CTU Danao Campus, where innovation meets tradition. Nestled in the heart of Danao City, our campus serves as a beacon of excellence in education and community engagement. With state-of-the-art facilities and a vibrant learning environment, we are dedicated to nurturing the next generation of leaders and innovators. Join us as we explore the highlights of our campus, showcasing the diverse programs and opportunities available to our students and the community.

About CTU Danao Campus

CTU Danao Campus is part of the Cebu Technological University system, committed to providing quality education through a variety of programs in engineering, technology, and management. Our campus fosters a culture of collaboration and creativity, encouraging students to engage in research, community service, and personal development. With a focus on hands-on learning and industry partnerships, we prepare our graduates for the challenges of the modern workforce.

CTU DANAO CAMPUS HIGHLIGHTS
New Administration Building
The newest addition to CTU Danao is the administration building. It serves as the main hub for campus management, housing the Campus Director and other important officials. This three-story building provides a better space for all stakeholders when dealing with CTU Danao.
CTU Danao Fablab
Funded by DTI, it offers advanced technology like 3D printing to support research and innovation in Engineering and Technology. It provides services like 3D printing, laser cutting, and training on digital design for MSMEs, faculty, and students. Launched in 2019, the Fablab also helps the community by offering programs to enhance digital skills for business, education, and other fields.
CTU Danao Hometel
Escape the busy city and visit this quiet 16-room spot within the University, where you can relax and take in a 360-degree view of the Northeastern Cebu landscape. You can also enjoy a swim in the new pool while watching the sunrise over the eastern coast.
Sports & Socio-cultural Center
The center is used for sports and social activities. Its open space is great for physical education classes, from dance to sports lessons. It can also host small events like meetings, seminars, workshops, and exhibits related to sports or culture. The building has two offices to handle sports and social-related matters.
